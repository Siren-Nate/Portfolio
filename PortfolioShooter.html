Multiplayer Third-Person Shooter Map Design


A much larger project than previously, designing and constructing a map for multiple players rather than just one. I went with the theme of an indoor park/arcade, and kept the lighting moody but with bright colored lights, similar to a laser tag arena. Unlike the relatively simple platformers, this represented multiple days of work.
